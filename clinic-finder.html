You have options — here's how to find the right one for you

There are no abortion clinics in the Gorge, but that doesn't mean you're out of options. Whether that's pills by mail or a clinic visit, this page walks you through it step by step.

Step 1
Find out how far along you are — it shapes everything

How many weeks pregnant you are decides which options are open to you, including whether you can get pills by mail. You don't need to figure this out alone: local Gorge providers offer free, confidential, all-options pre-appointments where supportive, non-judgmental staff confirm your gestational age and help you weigh your next steps — with no pressure and no commitment.

Not sure how far along you are? That's really common — you don't need to know before reaching out. A pre-appointment is exactly where you find out.

Step 2 — if under 11 weeks

Abortion medication by mail may be your best option

If you're under 11 weeks pregnant, abortion pills are safe, effective, and can be sent directly to your home — no travel required. This is especially worth considering if:

  • You're within the first 11 weeks of pregnancy
  • Travel to Portland or Vancouver is a barrier (distance, work, kids, cost)
  • You'd prefer privacy and care in your own home
  • You want to avoid wait times at a clinic
